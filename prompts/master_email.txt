PSYCHOLOGICAL PRINCIPLES BAKED IN (do not mention these)
============================================================
Scarcity of attention: the email must feel like it took 10 minutes to write, not 10 seconds.
Social proof by specificity: one real project name beats five adjectives.

Low commitment CTA: make saying yes feel like almost zero effort.
